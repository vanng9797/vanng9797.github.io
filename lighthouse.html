Light House

"Light House" is a group project that I participated in as a project organizer, character designer, concept astist.

Storyboard description: (1) A rugged seacoast, a lighthouse perched on a cliff near a cottage below, circa 1875, night. A worried girl studies a nautical map by the light of a gas lantern. Outside a storm rages. Her father is caught at sea in a small sailboat. (2) The girl hears a loud crash, looks outside up the hill and sees that the lighthouse window has shattered. A gust of wind blows out the light. (3) She hurries along the edge of the cliff with the lantern in pelting rain, climbs the spiral stairs and uses the lantern to re-light the Fresnel lamp. (4) Father sees the light and steers the boat homeward. Blend a product tag line onto the light beam.

In this project, I was in charge of storyboarding part (3), and character designs, revising and combining all parts together.
