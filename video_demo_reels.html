DEMO REELS

It has been three years since I first joined Youtaite community as an animator/ motion grapher; and Kumo is my nickname I go by.
These are Demo reels that I made as summaries of many projects that I have collaborated or personally worked on within the year of 2018 and 2019.

Youtaite world is a very creative community based on the fandom of Vocaloid, uniting anyone who appreciate Japanese Vocaloid music and arts. Youtaites with various specialties such as singing, illustrating, mixing, animating would get together as a group or individually make contents such as song covers, illustrations, videos and share them on Youtube. There are many online competitions called "Chorus Battle" from time to time, where Youtaite groups compete with each other. I have collaborated with many online friends all over the world, joined ~5 competitions and contributed ~15 entries. They will always be my best experiences as that I had as an amateur motion graphic artist.
